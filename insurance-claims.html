Windscreen Insurance Claims in New Zealand

We make insurance claims simple. We deal with all major NZ insurers, handle the paperwork, and invoice them directly — so you only pay your excess.

Which Insurers Do We Work With?

We accept and process claims from all the major New Zealand insurers:

  • AA Insurance — comprehensive policies usually cover glass with standard excess.
  • State Insurance — windscreen cover is often included; we bill State directly.
  • AMI Insurance — common for private vehicles; we handle AMI claims end-to-end.
  • Vero Insurance — popular for business and fleet vehicles; direct invoicing accepted.
  • FMG — rural and commercial specialists; we process FMG glass claims regularly.
  • Tower Insurance — standard comprehensive policies cover windscreen replacement.
Not sure if you're covered? Call us on 09 972-1091 with your rego and we'll check your policy quickly. Most comprehensive policies cover glass replacement.

How the Claim Process Works

1

Call Us First

Most NZ insurers let you choose your repairer. Call 09 972-1091 with your policy number and vehicle details. We'll confirm you're covered and what your excess is.

2

We Assess & Source Glass

We inspect the damage, confirm replacement is needed, and order the correct OEM-quality glass for your make and model. Common glass is usually in stock.

3

We Handle the Paperwork

We invoice your insurer directly and manage all claim paperwork. You usually only pay your excess. No chasing, no admin.

4

Mobile Fitting in Auckland

We come to you anywhere across Auckland — home, work or roadside. We fit to manufacturer spec, advise safe drive-away time, and leave your vehicle secure.

What About the Excess?

Most comprehensive policies include windscreen cover with a standard excess — typically between $50 and $200. Some policies offer reduced or zero excess for glass claims. We confirm your excess before we start, so there are no surprises.

Do I Have to Use the Insurer's Repairer?

No. In New Zealand you have the right to choose your own repairer. We are an independent specialist. We work with all insurers and invoice them directly. Your insurer cannot force you to use their preferred provider.
