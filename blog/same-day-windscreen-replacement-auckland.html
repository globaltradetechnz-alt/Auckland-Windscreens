Same-Day Windscreen Replacement in Auckland

How we get you back on the road the same day — what to expect and how to book priority mobile service anywhere in Auckland.

Can You Really Replace a Windscreen the Same Day?

Yes — for most common makes and models we can fit the glass the same day you call. We carry stock for popular vehicles and our mobile fitting team covers the entire Auckland region from Orewa to Pokeno. Call 09 972-1091 early in the day for the best chance of same-day fitting.

Which Vehicles Get Same-Day Service?

  • Common Japanese makes — Toyota, Mazda, Honda, Nissan, Mitsubishi: usually in stock.
  • Popular utes and SUVs — Ford Ranger, Toyota Hilux, RAV4, Mazda CX-5: high stock levels.
  • European makes — VW, BMW, Mercedes: often available, but some models need ordering.
  • Rare or classic vehicles — may need 24–48 hours to source the correct glass.

How the Same-Day Process Works

  1. Call us by 10am — we confirm your vehicle, damage and location.
  2. We check stock — if the glass is available, we book a mobile slot that day.
  3. Mobile fitting — we come to your home, work or roadside across Auckland.
  4. Safe drive-away — we tell you exactly when the adhesive is safe before you drive.

What If the Glass Is Not in Stock?

For specialist or rare glass, we order immediately and book you in the moment it arrives — usually within 24 hours. We keep you informed throughout so you are never left guessing.

Does Same-Day Cost More?

No. Our same-day service is standard. We do not charge extra for urgency. The price is based on your vehicle and glass type, not how fast you need it.

Fleet or rental vehicles? We prioritise fleet and commercial jobs because downtime costs money. Call 09 972-1091 and mention your fleet — we will fast-track the booking.
